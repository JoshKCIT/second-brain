# Research Impact Report: Singapore Keynote — Building a Second Brain (AI Adoption)

## Executive Judgment

This keynote is **strong governance-and-closure evidence** with **dangerous consumer-stack noise**. Dr Balakrishnan's personal agent story (nano-claw, WhatsApp, Neiman graph memory, Obsidian auto-wikis) must not be imported into Second Brain v1. The transferable material reinforces that **understanding and accountability stay human**, that **tools and deterministic checks beat model hype**, and that **hybrid LLM+rule-based pipelines** match the compiler metaphor. Reject messaging-channel agents and graph-memory-as-truth; experiment only on edge-local validation and explicit tool-assembly documentation.
