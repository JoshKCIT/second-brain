# Research Impact Report: My Simple Claude Cowork System for Normal People

## Executive Judgment

This tutorial contains **strong agent-instruction ergonomics** wrapped in a **consumer personal OS story** that must not become Second Brain's product definition. Jeff's constitution/state-law rule stacking, routing maps, disposable memory, session audit skills, lean root pointers, and voice-principle packs translate cleanly to Second Brain's governed documentation compiler when scoped to AGENTS.md shims, in-progress project stages, and approval-gated orientation—not Email HQ, personal finance workstations, Gmail connectors, or Notion command centers. One architectural pattern (hierarchical instruction stacking) is ready to adopt; six workflow patterns warrant controlled experiments adjacent to RC-018, RC-055, RC-058, and RC-130; six claims are rejected as scope drift or governance risk; two market/principle claims remain monitor-only.
