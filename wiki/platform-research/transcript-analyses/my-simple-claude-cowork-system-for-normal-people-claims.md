## Executive Judgment

This transcript is **high-value for agent instruction ergonomics** and **misaligned as a product blueprint**. Jeff operationalizes hierarchical rule stacking, routing maps, disposable session memory, session audit skills, lean root instructions with pointer resources, and voice-principle packs in a consumer "Co-Work OS" folder—patterns that translate to Second Brain's governed documentation compiler when scoped to **AGENTS.md shims**, **in-progress project stages**, and **approval-gated orientation files**, not personal life workstations. Reject Cowork/Notion as substrate, unbounded memory.md as canonical truth, auto Gmail/finance ingestion without approval, and consumer personal OS product scope. Adopt explicit hierarchical instruction stacking; experiment with routing maps, disposable orientation, session audit, lean root pointers, voice packs, and project subfolder rule stacking adjacent to RC-018, RC-055, RC-058, and RC-130.
